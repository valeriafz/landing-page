Our inspiration

Our trip to Iceland was a breathtaking journey through majestic landscapes, with snow-capped mountains, sparkling glaciers, and the serene northern lights. The crisp air, volcanic earth, and refreshing Icelandic springs inspired us to create Nordic Candles—infused with scents of fresh pine, sea breeze, and mineral-rich earth, capturing the essence of these magical places in every handcrafted piece.

Candle Making Process

Our candle-making process is a blend of artistry and craftsmanship, with years of experience in the back. We handpick the finest natural waxes and essential oils, carefully blending scents inspired by nature’s beauty. Each candle is poured by hand, ensuring precision and quality. The final touch is adding eco-friendly wicks, creating a warm, glowing atmosphere in every home.
